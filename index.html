My Story

Honesty and hard work are the core values that have guided me throughout my life, values deeply ingrained in me through my upbringing in a well-groomed environment in a government society in Taxila. My mother’s death, a turning point in my life, has shaped my character, and for nearly 12 years, I have strived to move forward while holding onto the lessons she imparted. This personal journey has led me to pursue my passion for software engineering, which I believe is my calling. Despite financial challenges, I have worked to support my education by learning web development and taking freelance opportunities. I’m committed to continuous growth, and sports like basketball, along with a disciplined exercise routine, help me stay focused and determined. My involvement in a community service course during my first semester strengthened my resolve to contribute to the world by solving real-world problems through technology. My biggest dream is to master the field of technology and create solutions that address the challenges people face daily. If I have the opportunity to work in Europe, I believe my skills will be highly valued by major companies, allowing me to make a meaningful impact globally.
